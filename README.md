# birthday-jamz

The goal is to generate a playlist for the members of a team

Input

1. the birth dates of the team members

e.g.:

1985-10-27
1996-12-23
1989-01-16

Process

Look for the top songs for on the birthday of each team member for every year since their birth

Output

1985-10-27
1986-10-27
1987-10-27
....
1996-10-27
1996-12-23
1997-10-27
1997-12-23
